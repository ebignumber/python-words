COMMANDS:
/compact: Merges letters in the letters list to shorten it
/easy: Makes it so that the letters in the letters list only contains letters in the words that haven't been guessed
/expand: Expands the letter list: (2E) becomes two E's in the list
/exit: Exits the program
/hint: Adds a hint to the puzzle, making a certain word easier to guess
/normal: Returns to the normal difficulty of the game
/reset: Resets the puzzle
/shuffle: Shuffles the letters list around
